About Beijing Technology and Business University

Established in 1950, Beijing Technology and Business University is a distinguished research-oriented university in Beijing. It boasts a multidisciplinary approach, encompassing science, engineering, economics, management, literature, law, and arts. With two modern campuses, Fucheng Road and Liangxiang, BTBU offers state-of-the-art facilities for teaching, research, and student life.

The university is committed to international exchange and cooperation, maintaining partnerships with institutions in over 50 countries. BTBU also offers various scholarships, including the Chinese Government Scholarship, International Chinese Language Teacher Scholarship, and Beijing Municipal Government Scholarship, and serves as a test center for HSK and HSKK.

Application Eligibility: Are You Ready for BTBU?

BTBU welcomes international students who meet the following criteria:

For Bachelor's Degree (Chinese-taught programs):

  • Must be a high school graduate
  • Required HSK Level 5 or above
  • Age between 18 and 25

For Master's/Doctoral Degree (Chinese-taught programs):

  • Master's applicants: Must hold a Bachelor's degree or equivalent
  • Doctoral applicants: Must hold a Master's degree or equivalent
  • Required HSK Level 5 or above
  • Master's applicants: Age not exceeding 35
  • Doctoral applicants: Age not exceeding 40

For Master's/Doctoral Degree (English-taught programs):

  • Master's applicants: Must hold a Bachelor's degree or equivalent
  • Doctoral applicants: Must hold a Master's degree or equivalent
  • Required TOEFL score of 80 or above, or IELTS overall score of 5.5 or above, or equivalent language proficiency recognized by BTBU. Native English speakers are exempt
  • Master's applicants: Age not exceeding 35
  • Doctoral applicants: Age not exceeding 40

Application Materials: What You Need to Prepare

To ensure a smooth application process, gather the following documents:

  1. BTBU Application Form: Complete and print the form generated from the online application system (https://btbu.17gz.org). Ensure it's filled in Chinese or English and includes a photo.
  2. Passport Scan: A clear scan of your passport's photo page and any relevant Chinese visa pages.
  3. Highest Degree Certificate/Study Certificate: A notarized copy in Chinese or English.
  4. Academic Transcripts: Notarized copies in Chinese or English.
  5. Foreigner Physical Examination Form: This form must be fully completed, including the physician's signature, hospital stamp, and your photo stamped over the seal. It should be valid for 6 months.
  6. Language Proficiency Certificate: HSK certificate for Chinese-taught programs; TOEFL/IELTS for English-taught programs.
  7. Resume/CV: An up-to-date curriculum vitae.
  8. Personal Statement: A compelling essay outlining your academic goals and reasons for applying.
  9. Study Plan/Research Proposal: A detailed plan for your intended studies or research.
  10. Two Recommendation Letters: From professors or employers.
  11. Opening Report: Required only for doctoral degree applicants.
  12. Notarized Certificate of No Criminal Record.
  13. Application Fee Remittance Slip: A copy of the payment receipt for the 420 RMB application fee.
  14. CSC Scholarship Application Form: If you are applying for the Chinese Government Scholarship, register at http://laihua.csc.edu.cn (BTBU agency code: 10011, Program Type: B).
