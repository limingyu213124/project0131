A Historic Foundation: Tongji's German Roots and Engineering Legacy

Tongji University‚Äôs story begins in 1907, founded as the Tongji German Medical School in Shanghai. This German heritage shaped a culture of precision and technical excellence. Today, as a Double First-Class university, Tongji leads China's effort to build world-class institutions, especially in Civil Engineering, Architecture, and Urban Planning (Our History).

Key Facts

  • Location: Shanghai, China
  • Established: 1907
  • Status: Project 985, Project 211, Double First-Class
  • Global Rankings: Civil Engineering ranked No. 1 globally by multiple rankings; Architecture & Urban Planning also top-tier
  • ESI: 15 academic fields in Top 1% globally

Funding Your Education: Scholarships at Tongji

1. Chinese Government Scholarship (CSC)

Available especially for graduates. Apply via CSC and select Tongji (Agency No. 10247).

See our CSC Scholarship Guide and CSC Type B Guide.

2. Shanghai Government Scholarship (SGS)

Full or partial tuition waiver for both undergraduate and graduate students.

3. Tongji University President Scholarship

Institutional scholarships to attract excellent international students.

Life on Campus and in Shanghai

Siping Campus offers a central urban experience; Jiading Campus hosts specialized colleges like Automotive Studies. Shanghai provides global culture, efficient transport, and endless professional opportunities.

Application Guide for Future Tongji Students

Apply via Tongji‚Äôs online application portal.

1. Confirm Eligibility

Review academic and language requirements for your target program.

2. Gather Documents

  • Passport information page
  • Notarized degree certificate(s) and transcripts
  • Language certificate (HSK / IELTS / TOEFL)
  • Personal statement / research proposal
  • Two recommendation letters (graduates)
  • Foreigner Physical Examination Record

3. Submit and Pay

Complete the online form, upload materials, and pay the fee.

4. Interview & Admission

Some programs interview candidates. Admitted students receive the admission package.
