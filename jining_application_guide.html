About Jining Medical University: A Tradition of Medical Excellence

Founded in 1952, JMU is a key public university in Shandong Province with a focus on medicine and supporting disciplines. The English-medium MBBS program is officially listed by the Ministry of Education for international students, ensuring national and international standards.

Key Facts

  • Location: Jining, Shandong
  • Established: 1952
  • Status: Provincial public medical university; MOE-listed for MBBS
  • Accreditation: WHO recognized; listed in WDOMS
  • Clinical Strength: 16 affiliated hospitals for extensive clinical training
