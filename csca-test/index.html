What is CSCA?

The China Scholastic Competency Assessment (CSCA) is a standardized examination designed to evaluate the academic readiness of international students applying to Chinese universities. It assesses proficiency in multiple subjects including Chinese language, mathematics, English, physics, and chemistry.
