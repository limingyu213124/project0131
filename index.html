Frequently Asked Questions

How does the China University Selector work?

Our tool uses a comprehensive 5-step interactive quiz based on real university data from China's top institutions. The algorithm considers university classifications (985/211/C9), admission requirements, tuition fees, scholarship opportunities, and program availability to provide personalized recommendations.

What data does the selector use?

We use comprehensive data from 45 top Chinese universities including university rankings (QS & THE), admission requirements (HSK levels, TOEFL/IELTS scores), tuition fees, scholarship information (CSC, Confucius Institute), and program availability across all major academic fields.

Is the China University Selector free to use?

Yes, our university matching tool is completely free and always will be. International students can get personalized recommendations based on real university data at no cost. We believe in making quality education guidance accessible to all students planning to study abroad in China.

How accurate are the university recommendations?

Our algorithm uses real data from Chinese universities including actual admission requirements, tuition fees, and scholarship availability. This ensures highly accurate matches based on your specific preferences for university type, language requirements, budget, and academic programs.
