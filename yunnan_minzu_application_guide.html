Yunnan Minzu University (YMU), established in 1951, is a comprehensive university with a rich history of educating students from all ethnic backgrounds. Located in the picturesque city of Kunming, YMU offers a wide range of programs across various disciplines. This comprehensive guide will help you navigate the application process for international students interested in pursuing their studies at this prestigious institution.

All information verified as of September 15, 2025.

About Yunnan Minzu University

Founded in 1951, Yunnan Minzu University is one of China's leading comprehensive universities with a strong focus on ethnic studies and multicultural education. The university is located in Kunming, the capital of Yunnan Province, known for its pleasant climate and rich cultural diversity. YMU has established itself as a key institution for international students seeking quality education in China.

The university offers programs across various disciplines including arts, sciences, engineering, medicine, and ethnic studies. With a commitment to multicultural education, YMU provides an ideal environment for international students to experience China's diverse cultures while pursuing their academic goals. Undergraduate Programs

Yunnan Minzu University offers a diverse range of undergraduate programs designed to provide students with a solid foundation in their chosen field of study. The admission process is designed to be fair and transparent, ensuring equal opportunities for all qualified applicants.

Admission Requirements

The primary principle for admission is based on the applicant's academic performance and qualifications. For international students, the university considers various factors including:

  • Academic transcripts from previous education
  • Language proficiency (Chinese or English depending on the program)
  • Health examination certificate
  • Personal statement and recommendation letters

Language Requirements

For most programs, there are no specific language requirements for international students. However, for programs taught in English, such as the English and non-universal language programs, a strong command of the English language is essential. Similarly, for the Japanese program, proficiency in either English or Japanese is required.

After enrollment, all students will take public foreign language courses, which include University English and University Japanese. For comprehensive visa information, check our student visa guide.

Postgraduate Programs

Yunnan Minzu University offers a variety of postgraduate programs, including doctoral and master's degrees. The application process for these programs is more specialized and requires a higher level of academic achievement.

Doctoral Programs

The doctoral programs at YMU are designed to cultivate high-level talent with a solid foundation in their chosen field and the ability to conduct independent and creative research. The admission process is based on an "application-assessment" system.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Hold a master's degree or be a graduating master's student
  • Strong academic record and clear research plan
  • Two letters of recommendation from professors in the applicant's field
  • Meet health and character requirements

Application Process

The application process for doctoral programs consists of two main stages:

  1. Online Application: Complete the online application through the China Graduate Admissions Information Network (https://yz.chsi.com.cn/). The application window is typically in May.
  2. Submission of Materials: Submit hard-copy materials including application form, academic transcripts, research plan, and recommendation letters.

International Student Programs

Yunnan Minzu University warmly welcomes international students to apply for its various programs. The university has a long history of enrolling international students and offers a supportive and enriching environment for them to pursue their academic goals.

Advantages of Studying at YMU

  • Scholarships: A variety of scholarships are available to international students, including the Chinese Government Scholarship, the Yunnan Provincial Government Scholarship, and university-specific scholarships.
  • High-Quality Education: YMU is committed to providing high-quality teaching and administration, ensuring that international students receive a top-notch education.
  • Cultural Immersion: The university offers a wide range of extracurricular activities and social practice opportunities, allowing international students to immerse themselves in the rich and diverse cultures of Yunnan.

English-Taught Master's Program in Pharmacy

For the 2025 September intake, YMU is offering an English-taught Master's Degree in Pharmacy. This program is designed for students who wish to pursue advanced studies in the field of pharmacy in an English-speaking environment.

Program Eligibility

  • Hold a bachelor's degree in Pharmacy, Biology, or Chemistry
  • Age between 18 and 35
  • English proficiency: IELTS 5.5+ or TOEFL 70+ or English-medium instruction certificate

Application Process

Applicants must complete the online application at https://ynni.at0086.cn/StuApplication/login.aspx before June 30, 2025. The application requires the submission of academic transcripts, a personal statement, and other supporting documents.

Program Fees

Tuition Fee: 15,000 CNY per year

Additional Fees: Accommodation, health insurance, and residence permit fees

Application Timeline and Deadlines

Understanding the application timeline is crucial for a successful application to Yunnan Minzu University. Here's a general timeline for international students:

September - December (Previous Year)

Research programs, prepare documents, and begin language proficiency tests if required.

January - March

Submit online applications and required documents. For pharmacy program, deadline is June 30.

April - June

University reviews applications, conducts interviews if necessary, and sends admission notifications.

July - August

Receive admission letters, apply for student visas, and prepare for arrival in China.
