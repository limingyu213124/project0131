üìÖ Published: September 9, 2025 ‚è±Ô∏è Read time: 10 minutes üìä Category: Life in China üë§ Author: China University Selector Team

If you're coming to Shenzhen University (SZU) as an international student, you'll most likely live on campus. SZU offers different room types on its Yuehai and Lihu campuses, with a significant price gap between campus options. This comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know about dormitory life at SZU, from room types and fees to practical tips for making the most of your campus experience.

Why This Guide Matters

Choosing the right accommodation is crucial for your study experience in China. SZU's dormitory system offers various options, but understanding the differences between campuses, room types, and associated costs will help you make an informed decision that fits your budget and lifestyle preferences.

This guide combines official SZU information with practical insights from current and former international students to give you a complete picture of what to expect when living on campus at Shenzhen University.

Campus Locations and Student Distribution

üè´ Yuehai Campus

The main campus where most international students are housed. Offers single and double room options with higher-end facilities and amenities.

  • Location: Main campus area
  • Room types: Single and double rooms
  • Target: International students seeking more privacy
  • Facilities: Modern amenities and closer to academic buildings

üè´ Lihu Campus

A secondary campus offering more affordable accommodation options, primarily four-bed rooms for budget-conscious students.

  • Location: Secondary campus area
  • Room types: Four-bed rooms
  • Target: Students seeking affordable options
  • Facilities: Basic amenities with communal living spaces

Room Types and Official Fees

üè† Yuehai Campus Options

Single Rooms: RMB 4,000-8,000 per semester per student

Double Rooms: RMB 2,400-4,000 per semester per student

  • Private or semi-private bathroom options
  • Air conditioning included
  • Basic furniture: bed, desk, wardrobe
  • Utilities (water/electricity) charged separately

üè† Lihu Campus Options

Four-Bed Rooms: RMB 750 per semester per student

  • Shared bathroom facilities
  • Communal living space
  • Basic furniture for each student
  • Utilities charged separately

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Note

All accommodation fees exclude utilities (water and electricity). Students typically pay an additional RMB 200-500 per month for utilities, depending on usage and room type.

Mandatory Fees and Registration Process

Student Tips and Recommendations

üí° Budget vs. Comfort Trade-offs

üîí Security and Organization

Quick FAQ

Q: Can I choose my roommate?

A: Roommate requests are sometimes possible, especially if you know another international student. Contact the housing office early to make arrangements.

Q: What if I'm not satisfied with my room assignment?

A: Room changes are possible but subject to availability and additional fees. Discuss options with the housing office after arrival.

Q: Are there laundry facilities in the dorms?

A: Yes, most dormitories have coin-operated washing machines. Some buildings also have dryers, but many students air-dry their clothes.

Q: Can I cook in my dorm room?

A: Cooking in individual rooms is generally not allowed due to safety regulations. However, some dormitories have shared kitchen facilities.

Q: What about transportation to and from campus?

A: SZU campuses are well-connected by public transportation. Campus shuttle buses run between different areas, and metro stations are within walking distance.
