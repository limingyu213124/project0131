A Guide to International Student Accommodation at Shenzhen University

Shenzhen University (SZU), a dynamic and rapidly growing institution in the heart of one of China's most vibrant cities, welcomes students from all over the world. For international students, finding the right place to live is a crucial part of settling into a new life abroad. This guide provides an overview of the accommodation options available to international students at SZU, focusing on the Liyuan International Student Apartments and other nearby choices.

For more information about studying at SZU, check our university selector tool and explore our Life in China section for additional living guides.

All information verified as of September 18, 2025.

Liyuan International Student Apartments

Shenzhen University offers dedicated housing for international students through the Liyuan International Student Apartments. These apartments are managed with a focus on providing a safe, comfortable, and convenient living environment. There are two main locations for these apartments: Houde Pinyuan and Yangguang Huayi.

Houde Pinyuan Apartment

Location and Convenience

The Houde Pinyuan Apartment is located at Building D, Houde Pinyuan, at the intersection of Xuefu Road and Nanhai Avenue in the Nanshan District. It is conveniently situated just 262 meters from the Liyemen (West Gate) of Shenzhen University, making it a short 5-minute walk to campus.

The apartment is also close to major shopping centers like Maoye Department Store, Raffles City, and Coastal City, as well as Nanshan Hospital and Shekou People's Hospital, providing excellent convenience for daily life.

Room Types and Facilities

The Houde Pinyuan Apartment offers a variety of room types, including:

  • Single occupancy rooms
  • Double occupancy rooms
  • Triple occupancy rooms
  • Quadruple occupancy rooms

All rooms are well-lit and come equipped with essential amenities such as:

  • Air conditioning
  • Water heater
  • Washing machine
  • Refrigerator
  • Beds, desks, and closets

Important Note: Students need to provide their own mattresses and bedding.

Security and Services

The apartment building features:

  • Hotel-style management with professional staff
  • Elevators for easy access
  • 24-hour security and access control
  • Professional cleaning and maintenance teams

Yangguang Huayi Apartment

Location and Convenience

The Yangguang Huayi Apartment is located in Block B of the Yangguang Huayi Building, at the intersection of Nanshan Avenue and Chuangye Road. It is approximately 486 meters from the Liyemen of Shenzhen University, an 8-minute walk to campus.

This location is also surrounded by shopping, dining, and recreational facilities, providing students with convenient access to daily necessities and entertainment options.

Room Types and Facilities

Similar to the Houde Pinyuan Apartment, the Yangguang Huayi Apartment offers:

  • Single rooms (some available as suites with sofa and coffee table)
  • Double rooms
  • Triple rooms
  • Quadruple rooms

The rooms are furnished and include:

  • Air conditioning
  • Water heater
  • Washing machine
  • Basic furniture

Important Note: Students are responsible for their own mattresses and bedding.

Off-Campus Accommodation

For students who prefer to live off-campus, the university's College of International Exchange provides guidance and a list of recommended rental options near the campus. These options vary in price, size, and style, and include apartments in areas like:

  • Nanshan Science and Technology Park
  • Qilin New Village
  • Guimiao New Village

The university provides contact information for landlords and real estate agents to assist students in their search for suitable off-campus housing.

Living Tips for International Students

Here are some helpful tips for international students living at SZU:

Before Arrival:

  • Contact the international office to confirm your accommodation assignment
  • Prepare essential items like bedding and personal belongings
  • Familiarize yourself with the campus and surrounding areas

After Arrival:

  • Complete registration and obtain your student ID
  • Set up local bank account and mobile phone service
  • Explore nearby shopping centers and restaurants
  • Join student organizations and activities

Cost and Payment Information

Accommodation costs at SZU vary depending on room type and location:

  • Single rooms: Higher cost but more privacy
  • Shared rooms: More affordable option for budget-conscious students
  • Payment: Usually paid per semester or academic year
  • Deposit: May be required upon check-in

For detailed pricing information, contact the SZU International Office directly.

Conclusion

Shenzhen University provides a range of accommodation options to suit the needs and preferences of international students. Whether you choose to live in the modern and convenient Liyuan International Student Apartments or explore off-campus housing, you can find a comfortable and welcoming place to call home during your studies in Shenzhen.
