About Dalian Medical University

Founded in 1947, DMU is a key provincial university dedicated to medical education and research, and among the earliest MOE-authorized institutions to run an English-medium MBBS (since 2004).

Key Facts

  • Location: Dalian, Liaoning
  • Established: 1947
  • Status: MOE-listed for English-taught MBBS; Provincial Key University
  • Accreditation: WHO recognized; listed in WDOMS
  • Clinical Power: Two comprehensive 3A-level affiliated hospitals

Why DMU for MBBS

MOE-Approved, Globally Recognized

Graduates pursue licensing pathways such as NMC (India), PMC (Pakistan), USMLE, and PLAB.

Strong Clinical Hospitals

  • The First Affiliated Hospital of DMU
  • The Second Affiliated Hospital of DMU

Both are top-ranked 3A hospitals with high patient volumes and modern facilities.
