Admission Requirements and Timeline

üìã Application Timeline

For the September intake, SZU typically follows this application schedule:

  • Application Period: January 1 - May 15 (check current year for exact dates)
  • Document Review: May - June
  • Interviews: June (remote or in-person)
  • Admission Notices: June
  • Visa Application: July - August
  • Enrollment: September

Required Documents

Prepare these essential documents for your SZU application:

  • Academic Documents: Degree certificate and official transcripts
  • Recommendation Letters: Two academic or professional references
  • Personal Statement: Approximately 800 words explaining your motivation
  • Language Certificates: HSK for Chinese programs, IELTS/TOEFL for English programs
  • Health Documents: Foreigner Physical Examination Form
  • Passport Copy: Valid passport information page
  • Photographs: Recent passport-style photos

Application Process and Procedures

üñ•Ô∏è Online Application System

SZU uses a comprehensive online application system for international students:

  1. Registration: Create an account on status.szu.edu.cn
  2. Program Selection: Choose your desired program and degree level
  3. Document Upload: Submit all required documents in digital format
  4. Application Fee: Pay the RMB 400 fee online or by designated transfer
  5. Submission: Review and submit your completed application

üìû Interview Process

After initial document review, selected applicants may be invited for interviews:

  • Interview Format: Remote (video call) or in-person options available
  • Interview Content: Academic background, research interests, motivation
  • Language Assessment: May include language proficiency evaluation
  • Preparation Tips: Research your program, prepare questions, test technology

Visa and Immigration Requirements

üìã Visa Application Process

After receiving admission, follow these steps for your student visa:

  1. Admission Documents: Receive Admission Notice and JW202 form from SZU
  2. Visa Application: Apply for X1 student visa at Chinese embassy/consulate
  3. Required Documents: Passport, JW202, Admission Notice, health certificate
  4. Visa Processing: Typically takes 5-10 business days
  5. Entry to China: Enter within 30 days of visa issuance
  6. Residence Permit: Convert X1 visa to residence permit within 30 days of entry

üè• Health Requirements

  • Physical Examination: Complete Foreigner Physical Examination Form
  • Medical Tests: Blood tests, chest X-ray, and general health check
  • Documentation: Form must be completed by authorized medical facility
  • Validity: Examination results valid for 6 months
