🎯 Why CSCA Matters for Your China Study Journey

📊 Standardized Assessment

The CSCA (China Scholastic Competency Assessment) is the key standardized test for international students applying to Chinese universities.

🏆 University Recognition

Top Chinese universities like Tsinghua, Peking University, and Fudan University require CSCA scores for admission.

💡 Comprehensive Testing

CSCA tests Chinese language, mathematics, English, physics, and chemistry - covering all essential academic skills.

📋 Quick Reference

⏰ Application Timeline

  • 12-18 months before: Start CSCA preparation
  • 6-12 months before: Take CSCA exam
  • Dec-Mar: Submit university applications
  • May-June: Receive acceptance letters
  • July-Aug: Apply for student visa
