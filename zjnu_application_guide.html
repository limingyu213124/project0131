About ZJNU

Founded in 1956 in Jinhua, ZJNU is a key provincial comprehensive university. Several disciplines (Chemistry, Materials Science, Engineering) rank in ESI Top 1% globally. ZJNU is renowned for international engagement and a large, diverse student body.

Why ZJNU

  • Teacher Education & Chinese Language: Leading TCSOL programs from immersion to BA/MA.
  • The Yiwu Advantage: Hands-on access to global trade for business/econ/Chinese students.
  • Global Community: Inclusive campus with strong support from the International Student Office.

Programs & Notable Disciplines

  • Education (Teacher Training): Curriculum design, pedagogy, educational psychology, classroom practice.
  • Chinese Language & Literature: Comprehensive track for advanced learners and future TCSOL teachers.
  • African Studies: One of China‚Äôs leading bases for Africa-focused research and cooperation.
  • STEM Highlights: Chemistry, Materials Science, and Engineering with ESI Top 1% performance.

Living in Jinhua (Next to Yiwu)

  • Cost of Living: Lower than coastal megacities; excellent value for students.
  • Connectivity: High-speed rail access to Hangzhou/Shanghai; convenient logistics to Yiwu.
  • Opportunities: Trade fairs, internships with traders/manufacturers, language practice with global merchants.

Scholarships

  • CSC Scholarship (graduates)
  • International Chinese Language Teachers Scholarship (TCSOL focus)
  • Zhejiang Provincial Government Scholarship
  • ZJNU President Scholarship

How to Apply

1. Check Eligibility

Non-Chinese citizen, healthy, with required academic background.

2. Prepare Documents

  • Online application form, passport copy
  • Notarized highest diploma & transcripts
  • Foreigner Physical Examination Form; No Criminal Record
  • Study plan & recommendation letters (for postgrad/scholarships)

3. Submit Online

Apply via ZJNU admissions and indicate scholarship interest where applicable.
