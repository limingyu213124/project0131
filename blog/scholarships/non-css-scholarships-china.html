Local Government Scholarships: Regional Powerhouses

Shanghai Government Scholarship (SGS)

Shanghai offers Type A full and Type B partial scholarships. Type A covers tuition, monthly living allowance, and medical insurance for graduate students; Type B provides tuition waivers for bachelor to PhD.

Apply via Shanghai universities during admission. Official info: SGS page.
