Expanded FAQs

How do I show feasibility without over‚Äëpromising?

Provide a modest, testable scope with milestones per semester. Note dependencies (lab access, compute time, HSK progression) and risk mitigations.

Mention 1‚Äì3 faculty by name only if you have read recent work and can cite overlap. Avoid implying prior commitment; keep it respectful and concise.
