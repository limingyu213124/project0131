Scholarships

1) Chinese Government Scholarship (CSC)

Full/partial coverage of tuition, accommodation, insurance, and stipend. Apply via Type A (Embassy Track) or Type B (University Track). Always confirm whether NBU accepts Type B in the current cycle.

2) Zhejiang Provincial Scholarship

Competitive provincial funding for high-achieving international students enrolled in Zhejiang universities.

3) NBU University Scholarships

Merit-based awards for new admits and continuing students. Check latest rules on NBU International College pages.

The cost of living is reasonable and public transport is efficient (metro, buses, high-speed rail).
