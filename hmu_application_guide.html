About Hainan Medical University (HMU): A Modern Institution in a Global Hub

Established in 1993 in Haikou, HMU is the only public medical university in Hainan, strategically located within the Hainan Free Trade Port with strong affiliated hospitals and research centers (About HMU).

Key Facts

  • Location: Haikou, Hainan Province
  • Established: 1993
  • Status: Provincial public medical university
  • Campuses: Chengxi, Longhua, Guilinyang
  • Accreditation: WHO recognized; listed in WDOMS

The English-Taught MBBS Program

Global Recognition and Accreditation

HMU's MBBS degree is recognized by councils such as PMC and NMC (India). Graduates can pursue licensing exams internationally.

Curriculum and Hands-On Training

  • Years 1‚Äì2: Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry, Pathology
  • Years 3‚Äì5: Internal Medicine, Surgery, OBGYN, Pediatrics with hospital practice
  • Year 6: Clinical internship

Clinical training occurs at top affiliated hospitals, notably the First Affiliated Hospital of HMU (3A-level).

Fees and Scholarships

  • Tuition: ~26,000 RMB/year
  • Accommodation: ~3,000 RMB/year (shared)

Hainan Provincial Government Scholarship

Attracts outstanding international students. Full scholarships may cover tuition, accommodation, insurance, and stipend; partial scholarships often cover full tuition.

Details and latest criteria: HMU International Admissions.

How to Apply to Hainan Medical University

1. Eligibility

18+ with completed secondary education and strong academics.

2. Required Documents

  • Application form, passport copy
  • Notarized diploma and transcripts
  • English proficiency proof (IELTS/TOEFL or equivalent)
  • Foreigner Physical Examination Form
  • No Criminal Record certificate
  • Financial guarantee statement

3. Submit Online

Apply via the HMU admissions portal; indicate scholarship consideration if available on the form.

Conclusion

HMU blends an English-taught MBBS, recognized accreditation, competitive tuition, and a tropical lifestyle. For aspiring doctors, it's a compelling choice in a rapidly developing international hub.
