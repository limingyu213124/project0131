For international students dreaming of studying in one of the world's most electrifying cities, Shanghai is a top destination. And at the heart of Shanghai's academic scene lies East China Normal University (ECNU), a prestigious institution renowned for its excellence in humanities, education, and sciences. As a key member of China's elite Project 985 and Project 211, ECNU offers a world-class education within a vibrant, cosmopolitan setting.

A Legacy of Wisdom and Innovation: The ECNU Story

Founded in Shanghai in October 1951, ECNU was among the first universities established in the PRC. Initially focused on training teachers, ECNU has evolved into a comprehensive research university while maintaining its strength in education. It is often called the "Garden University" for its beautiful campuses (About ECNU).

Designated as a Double First-Class University, ECNU is recognized for national importance and global aspirations.

Key Facts

  • Location: Shanghai, China
  • Established: 1951
  • Status: Project 985, Project 211, Double First-Class
  • Campuses: Minhang and Putuo
  • Discipline Strength: Education, Psychology, Geography, Chinese Language & Literature; 19 disciplines in ESI Top 1%
  • Global Community: 6,000+ international students from 130+ countries annually

Academic Excellence and Programs for a Global Audience

ECNU is comprehensive but shines in social sciences and humanities.

Strengths and Key Programs

  • Chinese Language and Culture: ECNU's Global Education Center offers top Chinese language programs from non-degree to BA/MA/PhD.
  • Education: National leader in educational research and practice.
  • Psychology & Cognitive Science: Home to a key national lab in brain and cognitive science.

Scholarships: Making Your Shanghai Dream a Reality

1. Chinese Government Scholarship (CSC)

ECNU is a designated university (Agency No. 10269) for this full scholarship covering tuition, accommodation, insurance, and stipend.

Learn more in our CSC Scholarship Guide.

2. Shanghai Government Scholarship (SGS)

Attracts top talent to Shanghai. Class A typically covers tuition and insurance; Class B usually covers tuition only.

3. ECNU President Scholarship

Tuition waiver for excellent new Master's or PhD students.

See details on ECNU's scholarship page.

Life on Campus and in Shanghai

  • Putuo Campus (Downtown): Historic charm with great city access; primary for Chinese language and some humanities.
  • Minhang Campus (Suburb): Modern, spacious; hosts most undergrads and many science/engineering faculties.

Both campuses offer modern libraries, dorms, sports facilities, and diverse dining. Shanghai offers the Bund, French Concession, world-class museums, and vibrant cuisine.

Application Process for ECNU

Applications are via ECNU's centralized online portal for international students.

1. Choose a Program

Browse catalogs and select your major.

2. Prepare Documentation

  • Passport info page
  • Graduation and degree certificates (notarized)
  • Official transcripts (notarized)
  • Language proof: HSK or IELTS/TOEFL
  • Study plan or research proposal (‚â•800 words for graduates)
  • Two recommendation letters (graduates)
  • Supervisor acceptance (if required for PhD)

3. Submit and Pay

Complete the portal submission and pay the 800 RMB application fee.

4. Track and Wait

Monitor status online. Admitted students receive Admission Notice and visa documents.

Conclusion

ECNU combines a top-tier academic reputation, especially in education and humanities, with the unmatched opportunities of Shanghai. With extensive English-taught programs and strong scholarships, it is an excellent destination for ambitious international students.
