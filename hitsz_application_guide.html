Application Requirements and Timeline

üìã Application Timeline

HITSZ follows a structured application process for international students:

  • Application Period: January 1 - May 15 (check current year for exact dates)

  • Admission Decisions: June - July
  • Visa Application: July - August
  • Enrollment: September

Scholarships and Financial Support

üèÜ Chinese Government Scholarship (CSC)

HITSZ participates in the prestigious CSC program:

  • Application Process: Through CSC online system and HITSZ scholarship instructions
  • Competition Level: Highly competitive, requires excellent academic record
