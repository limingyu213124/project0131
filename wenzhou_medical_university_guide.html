A Hub of Medical Innovation

Founded in 1958, WMU is a key provincial university approved by MOE for English-taught MBBS, emphasizing research and clinical practice.

Why Choose WMU for MBBS

  • Ophthalmology & Optometry strengths with leading clinical centers
  • English-taught MBBS for international students
  • Comprehensive clinical network for internships
