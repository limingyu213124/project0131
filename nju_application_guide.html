Documents Checklist

Online Application: Portal Flow

  1. Create account at NJU International Student Service System.
  2. Complete forms (personal info, education, language, program choice).
  3. Upload all documents (clear scans, correct format).
  4. Pay the application fee online and keep the receipt.
  5. Submit and track status via the portal.
