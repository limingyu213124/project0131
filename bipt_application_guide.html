About Beijing Institute of Petrochemical Technology

Beijing Institute of Petrochemical Technology is a distinctive university in Beijing, specializing in petrochemical engineering and related disciplines. It is dedicated to cultivating high-quality talents for the petrochemical industry and other sectors. BIPT emphasizes practical skills and scientific research, providing students with a strong foundation for their future careers.

Located in Beijing, the university offers a unique blend of specialized education within a bustling metropolitan environment, providing students with both academic rigor and cultural immersion. The institute focuses on hands-on learning and industry partnerships, making it an excellent choice for students pursuing careers in the energy and petrochemical sectors.

Application Process: Your Steps to BIPT

Applying to BIPT as an international student generally involves the following steps:

  1. Online Application: Complete the online application form through the designated international student application system. Ensure all required fields are accurately filled.
  2. Submission of Documents: Upload all necessary application materials as specified by the university. These typically include academic transcripts, degree certificates, passport copies, and language proficiency proofs.
  3. Review and Admission: The university will review your application. Successful applicants will receive an Admission Letter and the JW202 form, which are crucial for visa application.
  4. Visa Application: With your Admission Letter and JW202 form, apply for a student visa (X1 or X2) at the Chinese Embassy or Consulate in your home country.
  5. Registration: Upon arrival in China, register at BIPT by the specified date, bringing all original documents and other required items for verification.

Key Application Requirements (General)

Basic Requirements:

  • Citizenship: Non-Chinese citizen with a valid foreign passport
  • Health: In good health
  • Age: Generally between 18 and 30 for undergraduate programs, and up to 40 for postgraduate programs
  • Academic Performance: Good academic record
  • Other: No criminal record, financial guarantee

Education Requirements:

  • Undergraduate: High school diploma or equivalent
  • Master's: Bachelor's degree or equivalent
  • Doctoral: Master's degree or equivalent

Language Proficiency:

  • Chinese-taught programs: HSK Level 4 or above (specific score requirements may vary)
  • English-taught programs: IELTS 6.0 or TOEFL 80 (or equivalent) for non-native English speakers

Application Materials (General List)

To ensure a smooth application process, prepare the following documents:

  • Application Form for International Students (online submission)
  • Photocopy of passport (photo page)
  • Highest diploma (notarized photocopy)
  • Academic transcripts (notarized photocopy)
  • Language proficiency certificate (HSK, IELTS, TOEFL, etc.)
  • Personal statement or study plan
  • Two recommendation letters (for postgraduate applicants)
  • Physical examination form
  • Certificate of no criminal record
  • Financial guarantee statement
  • Application fee payment receipt

Important Notes

Application Deadlines:

Application deadlines vary by program and scholarship type. It is crucial to check the official BIPT international student website for the most up-to-date information regarding application periods for the current academic year.

Scholarships:

BIPT offers various scholarships, including the Chinese Government Scholarship and Beijing Municipal Government Scholarship. Eligibility and application procedures for these scholarships are detailed on the university's international student website.
